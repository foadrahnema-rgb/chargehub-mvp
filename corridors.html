EV Charging Corridors

Planning routes where optionality matters more than range on paper.
What is a corridor?
A corridor shows where EV charging options exist — and where they don’t.

Instead of optimizing for speed or charging curves, corridors focus on single points of failure and whether you still have options if a charger is down.
